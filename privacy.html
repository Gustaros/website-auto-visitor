Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2025

This extension, Website Auto Visitor, respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing history to any external servers.

What information do we collect?

None. All your scenarios and settings are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device.

Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required to automate your browsing scenarios. No data is sent to third parties.

Third-party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the GitHub Issues page.
